# Governance And Limitations

- This is a public-data personal portfolio project, not a production credit decisioning system.
- The final score is a ranking score for review prioritization, not a calibrated probability of default.
- Policy analysis is a scenario simulation. It does not estimate realized profit or regulatory capital impact.
- The 20x non-default weighting is described only as an AMEX competition sampling-adjusted scenario.
- Customer-level exposure, revenue, recovery, credit limit, and intervention outcome data are unavailable.
- The Top 17% result is a modeling sample cutoff with maximum simulated net benefit under stated assumptions, not an operational policy recommendation.
- Before any real-world use, the model would require calibration validation, out-of-time validation, fairness review, adverse-action governance, drift monitoring, audit logging, access control, and champion-challenger testing.
